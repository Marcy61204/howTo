Fried Egg Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of favorite bread
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 slices of favorite cheese (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • non-cooking spray or butter

Steps

  1. 1. Turn on stovetop on to medium heat
  2. 2. Spray skillet with nonstick cooking spray or butter. I prefer butter.
  3. 3. Crack eggs carefully into heated skillet. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. 4. When eggs are fully cooked to desired consistency, carefully flip the eggs.
  5. 5. Meanwhile, toast the desired bread.
  6. 6. Once eggs are done, place them on the toasted bread.
  7. 7. Add cheese, if desired.
  8. 8. Enjoy!

Chicken Salad Sandwich-Marcy's Way

Ingredients

  • 1 c. rotisserie chicken, chopped
  • 1/3 c. chopped celery
  • 1/3 c. chopped onion
  • 2 or 3 tbsp. mayo or salad dressing, whichever you prefer
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices of bread
  • Lettuce and tomato (optional)

Steps

  1. 1. Mix together the first five ingredients
  2. 2. If you would like, you can toast your bread, but is entirely optional.
  3. 3. Spread the chicken mixture onto one bread slice.
  4. 4. If desired, add your tomato and lettuce.
  5. 5. Put the second slice of bread on top.
  6. 6. Enjoy!

Chili Mac

Ingredients

  • 3 tbs. olive oil
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 c. peppers and onions, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 pkg. chili seasoning mix
  • 1 c. water
  • 1 jar of preferred tomato sauce
  • 1 c. of sharp cheddar cheese or Mexican cheese, whichever you prefer

Steps

  1. 1. Heat skillet to medium-high heat. Add oil.
  2. 2. Add peppers and onions to skillet and saute until soft and translucent
  3. 3. Meanwhile, boil pasta according to package directions.
  4. 4. Once peppers are ready and ground beef to skillet.
  5. 5 Add seasonings to ground beef, and saute beef until browned. Drain.
  6. 6. Add next four ingredients and bring to a boil.
  7. 7. Add more seasonings if you wish.
  8. 8. Once boiling, reduce heat to a simmer. Stir occasionally.
  9. 9. When pasta is al dente, drain, and add to meat mixture.
  10. 10. Stir together and let flavors marry. Add cheese and let melt.
  11. 11. Serve warm and enjoy!
